Our SErvices

Hello, we're ClearWater Pool Solutions, and we strive to ensure proper circulation and energy-efficient operations for your pool. With our services, we we want to keep your pools clean and make sure they are chemically-balanced!

Inspections

Call us for inspections when the pool is not functioning normally. We check to monitor the backwash filters, chemical levels, and can perform initial inspections for new home owners. We also can install any new equipment.

repairs

For any existing pool damages, we provide algae treatment, acid washes, replacements for pups, filters, along with fixing timers, plumbing, leaks, and heaters.

Weekly Service

We recommend getting your pool serviced on a weekly basis to maintain crystal-clear water. During our cleanings, we manage debris and tile cleanings, we vaccuum the pool and spa areas, and handle waste for the skimmers and baskets.

maintenance

To keep pools clean between seasons, we also offer winterizing and summerizing services.
